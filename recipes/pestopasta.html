Pesto Pasta

Description

This supper couldn't be easier. Make our filling pesto pasta recipe for a quick, family-friendly dish you can throw together with storecupboard ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 500g pack of spaghetti, fresh or dried
  • 2-3 tbsp pesto from a jar
  • 50g parmesan (or vegetarian alternative), shaved or grated

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in a large pan according to pack instructions.
  2. Drain well, then return to the pan with the pesto and half the Parmesan
  3. Toss well, then tip into a serving bowl and sprinkle with the remaining Parmesan.
